## Common Mistakes & Watchouts
- Creating checklists too long or complicated — becomes unusable on-site.  
- Missing **critical trade dependencies** (e.g., POP before electrical points).  
- Not including **material quality verification** (brand, grade, quantity).  
- Ignoring **safety and cleanup checks** at each stage.  
- Using checklists only once, instead of updating as work progresses.

## Tips From Experience
- Keep **separate checklists per trade** for clarity.  
- Highlight **critical milestones** in bold or color.  
- Use **digital tools** (Google Sheets, Notion) to allow remote access and real-time updates.  
- Always **verify items physically** even if the contractor marks them done.  
- Include **photo verification column** to avoid disputes later. 
- Maintain a **master checklist copy** for final handover and warranty claims.
